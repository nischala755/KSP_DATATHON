# KSP Datathon 2026 template analysis

## Structural findings

- Format: Microsoft PowerPoint `.pptx`
- Canvas: 16:9 widescreen (`9144000 x 5143500` EMU)
- Total slides: 16
- Embedded media assets: 3 PNG files
- Primary content style: black background with white type
- Accent system: teal, cyan, blue, violet, yellow and magenta gradients
- Persistent header: Karnataka State Police on the left; Zoho and H2S on the right
- Visual tone: technology-forward, high contrast, minimal, suitable for diagrams and dashboard screenshots

## What the template rewards

The sequence is designed to assess five things:

1. **Problem clarity:** can the team state the operational pain in one sentence?
2. **Differentiation:** is this more than a generic chatbot?
3. **Prototype evidence:** are there real screenshots, measurable behavior and a working link?
4. **Catalyst adoption:** is the deployment genuinely on Zoho Catalyst rather than merely planned?
5. **Execution credibility:** does the team distinguish implemented features from future scope?

## Recommended density

- One core message per slide
- No more than 35–45 body words on visual slides
- No more than six feature cards on a slide
- Minimum 22 pt body type and 30 pt section headers
- Use the notes in the content guide for spoken details instead of shrinking text
- Prefer evidence cards, diagrams and screenshots over paragraphs

## Template-preservation rules

- Do not remove the Karnataka State Police, Zoho or H2S branding.
- Do not alter the supplied header or bottom gradient strip.
- Do not place content closer than approximately 0.5 inches to the edges.
- Keep all body content below the header band.
- Retain slide 16 as the official closing screen.
- Use the supplied black background rather than adding white content panels.
